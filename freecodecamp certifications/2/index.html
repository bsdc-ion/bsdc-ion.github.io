Here is a timeline of Adobe Flash Player

  • 1996 - Launch of the first version under the name "Macromedia".
  • 2000 - More websites started using it.
  • 2007 - First version under the name of "Adobe Flash Player".
  • 2007 - First time seen on MacOS.
  • 2011 - Flash Player now supported by mobile devices.
  • 2015 - Last version that includes big changes.
  • 2020 - Last version that was launched globally.
  • 2021 - Flash was completly killed.
