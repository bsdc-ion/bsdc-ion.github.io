Welcome from the Headteacher

Lewis Fenn Griffin

Welcome to Paget High School, where we aim to provide an excellent education for all of our students, where we celebrate diversity and strive to foster an environment where every young person can flourish, academically, socially and emotionally.

At Paget, our commitment to ensuring success is reflected in our goal of disruption free lessons, our dedicated pastoral team and rich professional development for staff. It is rooted in our belief that every individual possesses unique talents and potential waiting to be unleashed.
